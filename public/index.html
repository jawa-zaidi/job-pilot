      🖱️ Browser auto-apply

      Opens a job's application page in your own Chrome, so you're logged in as yourself and the tailored CV is ready — then you click Apply. JobPilot never submits for you.

      Deliberately conservative. Sudden spikes in activity are the main thing that gets accounts flagged.
      ⚠️ LinkedIn Easy Apply — read this

      LinkedIn's Terms forbid automation. Even careful use carries real risk:

      • Temporary action blocks (can't apply/connect for hours–days)
      • A permanent account restriction or ban
      • Loss of your network, messages and history on that account

      JobPilot lowers the risk by using your real logged-in Chrome (no bot login), never running headless, pacing actions with human-like delays and the daily cap, and leaving the final click to you — but it cannot eliminate the risk.

      Greenhouse, Lever and other company career-page forms have no login and carry none of this risk — they work with just the toggle at the top.
